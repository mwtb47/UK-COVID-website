Cases

A case is an individual who has tested positive for the SARS-CoV-2 virus. If someone tests positive more than once in a 7 day period, they are only counted once. The date of the case is reported in two ways:
- specimen date (date of the test)
- published date (date the test result is included in totals)

More information about the reporting of cases can be found here.
